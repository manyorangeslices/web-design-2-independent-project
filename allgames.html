Top 5 Games

Nintendo Games

Below are the few games I have played on Nintendo consoles.

Link's Awakening 1993

An action/adventure game feauturing Link, a green clad boy that must aquire different instruments to help awaken the Wind Fish that allows him to go home.

Played On
Nintendo Gameboy Advance
Rating
4/5
Pros
Really nice music
Nostalgic and calming gameplay
Iconic characters
Cons
Complicated dungeons
Repetitive gameplay
Confusing mechanics

Kirby's Dream Land 1992

Follow the adorable Kirby through Dream Land as he aquires abilities from enemies and helps retrieve stolen food and Sparkling Stars from the nefarious King Dedede.

Played On
Nintendo Gameboy Advance
Rating
5/5
Pros
Super easy to play
Rewarding gameplay
Fun enemies and abilities
Cons
Somewhat repetitive gameplay
Super linear story
Old graphics make for confusing gameplay at times

Super Mario Land 1989

In Super Mario Land, Mario travels to Sarasaland to save Princess Daisy from Tatanga, an evil spaceman. Mario braves submarines and airplanes to overcome enemies

Played On
Nintendo Gameboy Advance
Rating
2/5
Pros
Nostalgic
Simple reward system
Straightforward, simple
Cons
Difficult gameplay (for a 6 year old)
No save points in levels
Old graphics make for confusing gameplay at times
